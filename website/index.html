About Me

Hey there! I'm Daniel Ku, and I am currently a second year data science student at Northeastern University! I've had so much fun during my almost two years in college so far, as I would have never thought I would be involved in so many different clubs. I'm currently involved in the Asian Student Union and campus radio station. With the Asian Student Union, I am on a committee for the mentorship program called ASU Minis! On this committee, we try to acclimate new students, both first year and transfer undergraduate students, to the Boston campus by pairing them to upperclassmen students based on majors, hobbies, and interests! It's been such a great time making such meaningful pairings and seeing how these students are grown in just a couple months into the school year! I'm also involved in the campus radio station called WRBB! I broadcast Northeastern basketball games that happen here in Boston! This is something new that I've been trying, and I've been having such a blast doing this. It combines both my knowledge of statistics and communication! I've grown up watching all the four major sports in America: basketball, baseball, football, and ice hockey. This has been a great way to stay involved in sports and meet new people!

Feel free to check out where my interest in music has taken me in my life in the Music section! I'm sure you'll be surprised!
